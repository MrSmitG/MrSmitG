After Troy, the sea remembers every sin. Sail as Odysseus through gods, monsters, and memory — until Ithaca answers.

Inspired by Homer & the epic spirit of Nolan’s Odyssey

How to Play

  • WASD or Arrows — Move Odysseus
  • E or Enter — Talk / Interact / Advance dialogue
  • Space — Strike with your bronze blade
  • Esc — Pause

Each island is a chapter. Listen to Athena. Survive Poseidon’s wrath. Return to Penelope.
